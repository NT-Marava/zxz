Our Ministry

I am the founder and president of Gathering Of Believers Int Ministry. This ministry is not just an organization, but a passionate pursuit of God's kingdom on earth. It is a calling that has been placed upon my heart to gather believers from all walks of life, united in their faith and dedication to the Gospel.


Gathering Of Believers International Ministry, under the visionary leadership of Prophet Givens Chizenga, is a dynamic and thriving ministry that relentlessly pursues its mission to bring God's light to the world. Anchored in the foundational principles of soul winning, preaching the Gospel, and restoring hope to God's people, this ministry has become a powerful force of transformation and spiritual revival.

In the face of unprecedented challenges brought about by the Covid-19 pandemic in 2019, Gathering Of Believers Int Ministry did not waver in its commitment to advancing the Kingdom of God. Despite restrictions on gatherings and lockdown measures, Prophet Givens Chizenga's unwavering dedication led him to personally visit families, going from home to home to encourage, pray for, and minister healing to the sick. With an unwavering faith in the power of God, he travelled great distances to reach every family and every individual, bringing hope and restoration in a time of great uncertainty.

As the years passed, Gathering Of Believers Int Ministry experienced exponential growth and expansion. In the year 2023, guided by the leading of the Holy Spirit, Prophet Givens Chizenga made the decision to return to his home country of Zimbabwe. The Prophet's homecoming was a resounding success, and in August 2023, Gathering of Believers Zimbabwe Branch was established in Harare, specifically in the Seke Chitungwiza area.

Since its inception, the Zimbabwe branch has experienced remarkable growth, welcoming new members with open arms each service. As a testament to their commitment to the spiritual well-being of individuals, Gathering of Believers Zimbabwe Branch extends an open invitation to all to come and worship, experience the presence of God, and partake in the transformative power of His Word.
