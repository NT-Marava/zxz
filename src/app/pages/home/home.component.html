About

Driven by the very things that move the heart of God, Prophet Givens Chizenga carries a powerful Prophetic mantle upon his life, illuminating the path of righteousness for believers around the world.

I am Prophet Givens Chizenga, and I am delighted to share my journey of faith with you. This website is a glimpse into my life as a believer, prophet, and minister of God's Word. From an early age, I heard the voice of God calling me to be a vessel for His glory and a messenger of hope and salvation. With unwavering faith and a deep conviction in the power of the Gospel, I have dedicated my life to proclaiming God's truths and guiding others on their spiritual journey.

As a believer, I stand firm in my trust in God and His Word. Through His grace, I have witnessed countless lives transformed and restored. I have been blessed with insights and revelations from God. It is my joy to share these divine messages with you, unveiling the mysteries of His Word and helping you navigate the challenges of life with faith and strength.

Work With Me

As a Man of God, I offer a range of services to support and guide believers on their spiritual journey. With a deep understanding of the Word of God and a profound Prophetic anointing, my mission is to bring hope, healing, and transformation to individuals seeking a closer relationship with God.

Through my ministry, I am committed to providing spiritual guidance and support to those who desire to grow in their faith. With a heart full of compassion and a keen sense of discernment, I offer personalized services that cater to the unique needs of each individual.

For those desiring a more personalized experience, I offer one-on-one sessions where we can dive deeper into your specific needs. During these sessions, you will have the opportunity to receive specific prophetic insights, spiritual guidance, and personalized prayer. Through the anointing upon my life, I will bring clarity, encouragement, and direction to your situation. These sessions are designed to empower you to walk in your God-given purpose, overcome obstacles, and experience the abundant life that God has in store for you.

If you are in need of prayer, I invite you to reach out to me and my dedicated prayer team. We believe in the power of intercession and standing in faith with you. Whether you require prayers for healing, breakthrough, deliverance, or any other specific need, we are committed to lifting up your requests before the Lord. Your prayer requests will be handled with utmost care and confidentiality. Submit your prayer requests through the provided channels, and rest assured that we will fervently pray for God's intervention in your life.

I, Prophet Givens Chizenga, am here to provide compassionate and biblically-based counseling services to individuals facing various challenges and seeking guidance. If you are dealing with personal struggles, relationship issues, or spiritual dilemmas, I offer you a safe and confidential space to share your concerns. With a heart full of empathy and wisdom rooted in God's Word, I will actively listen, provide guidance, and offer practical solutions to help you navigate through your difficulties.
